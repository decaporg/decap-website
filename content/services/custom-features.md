Empower your editorial workflow with feature work tailored to your exact requirements. Decap maintainers can help deliver enhancements that fit your project architecture and release strategy.

### What we offer

#### Core enhancements

For feature requests that fit the product roadmap, we can collaborate on implementation aimed at inclusion in Decap CMS core.

#### Feature plugins

When a feature is project-specific or better maintained separately, we can build it as a standalone NPM package.

#### Fork customization

For teams running a Decap fork, we can develop and integrate enhancements directly into your maintained codebase.

### Process

1. Consultation

We review goals, constraints, and expected outcomes.

2. Proposal

You receive a scope proposal covering deliverables, timeline, and an estimate.

3. Development and testing

Implementation is delivered transparently, with issue tracking and iterative validation.

4. Delivery

The feature is shipped in the agreed target: Decap core, plugin package, or your fork.

### Why teams choose this

- Tailored delivery by maintainers who know Decap internals.
- Transparent pricing based on project complexity and scope.
- Clear implementation path from idea to production-ready outcome.

### Engagement model

Pricing is per project. To discuss fit and receive a scoped offer, [contact the Decap maintainers](/contact/?topic=services).
