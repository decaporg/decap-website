Get your team productive quickly with setup guidance, focused workshops, and follow-up support tailored to your stack and publishing model.

### Typical onboarding scope

- Setup assistance for your repository and CMS configuration.
- Two focused training workshops.
- Follow-up Q&A session for rollout questions.

### Why onboarding helps

- Reduce ramp-up time for developers and editors.
- Align your workflow with Decap best practices.
- Build confidence before production launch.

This service is provided by Decap partner PM d.o.o. [Learn more about onboarding](https://www.p-m.si/en/code/decap/#onboarding).
