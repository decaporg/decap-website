Decap CMS Expert Services are focused on implementation work for teams that want expert help getting a project launched, extended, or handed off cleanly.

## Development of Custom Features

Sponsor the development of a custom Decap feature or priority bug fix tailored to your needs. This service is delivered by Decap maintainers.

Custom feature development and priority bug-fix work are independent offerings and are not bundled into Decap Turbo plans.

To discuss scope and fit, [contact the Decap maintainers](/contact/?topic=services).
