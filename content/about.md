## Our mission

Decap CMS exists to give publishers, editors, and developers a content system they can own. The project is built around a simple idea: your content should stay in your repository, your workflow should stay flexible, and your team should not have to trade independence for a usable editing experience.

## How the project is maintained

Decap CMS is a free and open-source project maintained in the EU with contributors from around the world. Core maintenance and long-term product direction are led by independent Decap maintainers, with community contributors helping improve the CMS, documentation, and ecosystem.

We keep governance lightweight and practical: discussion happens in the open, source code lives on GitHub, and the public roadmap is shaped by community feedback, maintenance priorities, and partner-backed investment in the project.

## What we value

- Free and open source forever.
- Independent stewardship instead of platform lock-in.
- Privacy-respecting tools for teams that publish on the open web.
- Sustainable maintenance backed by contributors, sponsors, and service work.

## Proof of trust

Decap CMS is used by teams that want a Git-based workflow without giving up editorial usability. The project is supported by a growing contributor base, public sponsors, and commercial partners that fund ongoing maintenance.
